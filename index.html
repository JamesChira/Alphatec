About Us

ALPHATEC REFRIGERATION AND AIR CONDITIONING is East Africa’s fastest growing heating, ventilation and air conditioning company based in Nairobi.

We specialize in all aspects of refrigeration and heating including supply and installation of air conditioning units, mechanical ventilation, With our friendly, courteous and professional staff, we aim to satisfy all the HVAC needs of our clients while delivering the most timely, cost effective and comprehensive same day service.

Whether you need new air conditioning units installed in your premises, repair problematic A/C units or have your existing units regularly and properly serviced and maintained by knowledgeable technicians, ALPHATEC AIRCONDITIONING LTD. is here, ready to serve you.

Alphatec Refrigeration & Air Conditioning targets both commercial and domestic market. We know that quality service is never an incident, it is always a result of high intentions, sincere effort, intelligent directions and skillful execution. It represents a wise choice of many alternatives. Our aim is to ensure a satisfied clientele by ensuring that the customers get effective and professional services from our highly trained and qualified workforce hence maintaining a level of customer satisfaction rarely matched.

Company Profile

Alphatec Refrigeration & Air Conditioning

Background•

Alphatec Refrigeration & Air Conditioning was founded and registered under the business act (cap 499 section 14) with a vision of raising the bar of excellence and providing quality services in refrigeration and air conditioning field.

Our Mission•

Alphatec Refrigeration & Air Conditioning aims to design, install, service, maintain cost effective, customized modern refrigeration and air conditioning services to all our clients at affordable prices.

Our Vision•

The company’s long-term vision is to be more creative, understanding and more customers focused thus developing a long and healthy working relationship.

Our Goal•

Alphatec Refrigeration & Air Conditioning targets to be an exceptional company giving our clients quality affordable services customized and tailored to suit their diverse needs.

Operations•

We are now operational throughout east Africa region.This has been achieved by providing quality services to our esteemed clients and hence attracting demand for our services. Some of our clients include:Hotels,super markets,shopping malls,factories,telecommunication firms,domestic air conditioning clients and car air conditioning,offices and Airports.
